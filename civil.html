Civil engineering
At Helmas Company, we offer comprehensive civil engineering services that cater to a wide range of infrastructure and development needs. Our team of experienced engineers and project managers is dedicated to delivering high-quality, sustainable solutions that meet the unique requirements of each project. Our services include:
    Structural Engineering: Our structural engineering services include the design and analysis of buildings, bridges, and other structures. We use advanced techniques and materials to ensure the safety and durability of our constructions.
    Infrastructure Development: We specialize in the planning, design, and construction of infrastructure projects such as roads, bridges, and highways. Our expertise ensures that these projects are completed efficiently and to the highest standards.
    Water Resources Engineering: We provide solutions for water supply, drainage, and flood control systems. Our services include the design and implementation of water treatment plants, irrigation systems, and stormwater management systems.
    Geotechnical Engineering: Our geotechnical engineering services involve the study of soil and rock mechanics to ensure the stability and safety of construction projects. We conduct thorough site investigations and provide recommendations for foundation design and earthworks.
    Environmental Engineering: We are committed to sustainable development and offer environmental engineering services that include waste management, pollution control, and environmental impact assessments. Our goal is to minimize the environmental footprint of our projects.
    Project Management: Our project management services ensure that all aspects of a project are coordinated and executed efficiently. We oversee the planning, design, construction, and maintenance phases to ensure timely and cost-effective completion.
Helmas Company is dedicated to providing innovative and reliable civil engineering solutions that enhance the quality of life and contribute to the development of Tanzania. Trust us to deliver projects that are not only functional but also sustainable and resilient.
